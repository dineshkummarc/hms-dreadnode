KNOW THE RULES TO STAY ALIVE

This probably isn't your first rodeo, but there are some things you need to know to make it through the next round of attacks.

First, you have to register yourself. This let's you get command of your fleet, but also makes sure you don't get any of that nasty friendly fire that's been going around.

Once you've registered, you will be presented with a game board. Scroll down for your game pieces. These pieces represent the proud women and men under your command. Treat them with respect.

And you treat them with respect by clicking and dragging the ships in strategic locations under the "Pray for Your Fleet" Board.

The fleet are already in vertical positions, perfect for offensive strikes.

However, if you want to go more pansy, protectionist methods then I guess you got enough hugs from your momma.

To change the orientation of the ships, press "R" while dragging the ships onto the game board.

When your fleet is positioned, click the Ready button to enter the operations' field.

At this moment, your fleet is set and you are free to fire on the enemy.

By firing on the enemy, go to the "May Your Aim Be True" Board and click on the pegs.

On this board you take turns firing against a computer's artificial intelligence or your friend over the Internet. Or maybe it's your friend's artificial intelligence? I get those confused.

Once you've secured victory and safety for your those under your command, you will be shown a report of how great you did.

If you don't win, you probably were going to die of dysentery on the trail anyway.

And with that, that's all you need to know, soldier. May the wind be at your back.
